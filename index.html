About CrediQuest

CrediQuest is a personal project built to answer one simple question: which card should you use right now?

It covers 16 popular Canadian credit cards across 25 spending categories and 629 stores. Go to Settings, select the cards you own, and search by store name or spending category to instantly see which card earns you the most.

Built with vanilla JS, Supabase, and hosted as a PWA — installable directly from Safari to your home screen.

Contribute

Found a missing store, wrong multiplier, or a card that should be added? Contributions are welcome.

  • Missing store — let me know the store name, its MCC code, and which reward category it falls under.
  • Wrong multiplier — share the card name, category, and the correct multiplier with a source.
  • New card — provide the card name and its multipliers across all 25 categories.

Reach out via the GitHub repository or email linked in the project readme.

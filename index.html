About CrediQuest

CrediQuest answers one question: which card should you use right now?

It covers 16 popular Canadian credit cards across 25 spending categories and 629+ stores. Go to My Cards, select the cards you own, then search by store or category to instantly see which card earns you the most.

Built with vanilla JS + Supabase, and installable as a PWA directly from Safari.

Contribute

Found a missing store, wrong multiplier, or a card that should be added?

  • Missing store — store name, MCC code, and reward category.
  • Wrong multiplier — card name, category, correct multiplier, and source.
  • New card — card name and multipliers across all 25 categories.

Reach out via the GitHub repository or email in the project readme.
